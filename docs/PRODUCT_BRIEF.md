# Product Brief

## Theme

Omni Inbox is a unified social support workspace for LINE, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram.

## Problem

Customer conversations are fragmented across platform-specific inboxes. Support teams lose context, ownership, and response visibility when they switch tools. Omni Inbox normalizes each platform into one conversation model while retaining its source identity.

## Showcase Scope

- Connect multiple accounts across three messaging platforms.
- Verify and ingest signed LINE and Meta webhook events.
- Reply through the correct provider connector.
- Route work using account permissions, assignment, status, priority, and tags.
- Preserve internal notes and generate handoff context.
- Surface realtime workload and cross-channel operational insights.
- Demonstrate the complete workflow locally without production credentials.

## Product Principle

The inbox is platform-agnostic; connectors are platform-specific. Agents learn one workflow, while the backend owns authentication, verification, payload normalization, and delivery differences.

## Success Criteria

- A reviewer understands the product within the first screen.
- A reviewer can compare conversations from all three platforms, switch user identities, and see access isolation.
- A reviewer can connect demo credentials and complete an inbound-to-outbound message loop.
- A developer can add another connector without redesigning the inbox domain model.
