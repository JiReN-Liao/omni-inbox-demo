# Roadmap

## Showcase Complete

- Unified LINE, Messenger, and Instagram inbox.
- Signed webhook ingestion and connector-aware replies.
- Account-level permissions, assignment, status, tags, notes, and priority.
- Realtime refresh, insights, handoff summaries, bilingual UI, and demo credentials.

## Team Pilot

- Postgres persistence and real organization authentication.
- Facebook Login for Business onboarding.
- Encrypted connector secrets and credential rotation.
- Media messages, reactions, story replies, and richer customer profiles.
- Search indexing, saved views, templates, and team mentions.

## Production

- Queue-backed webhook processing, delivery retries, and dead-letter recovery.
- Distributed idempotency, provider rate-limit handling, and message-window policies.
- Service-level reporting, quality review, exports, billing, and workspace settings.
- Centralized logs, traces, alerts, security review, and compliance controls.

## Next Connector

Add WhatsApp Business using the same Meta foundation, then extract a formal connector interface once the fourth provider proves the shared contract.
